**The rupee closed at a fresh low of ₹96.335 to the dollar, and Nifty IT closed up 2.43%.** That was the cleanest single-driver rotation on the tape. The OpenAI-and-Anthropic deal-share question on IT is not resolved; only the cohort's cascade-magnitude has unwound.
