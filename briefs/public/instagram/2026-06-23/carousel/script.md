## Card 2 — The WHY (the durable concept)
- kicker: Why it matters
- headline: Cheaper oil eases everything
- why: India buys roughly 85% of its oil from abroad. When the war premium drains out of crude, the import bill, inflation and the rupee all ease at once, and bond yields with them. This is the single most consequential thing that happened today, far more than the 0.37% on the screen.
