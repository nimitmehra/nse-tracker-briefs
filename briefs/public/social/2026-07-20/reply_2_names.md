A few names worth knowing from Monday.

The offsets to the bank drag were strong. UltraTech Cement, the biggest to report, grew profit 17% to ₹2,604 crore on solid volumes and rose 1.5%. Among state lenders, Punjab National Bank's profit more than tripled, up 213%, and the stock jumped 5.66%, while Indian Overseas Bank grew profit 46% and rose 3.4%.

Not every good result was rewarded. Karur Vysya Bank grew profit 45% yet fell 2.84%, caught in the same private-bank de-risking despite a clean quarter, a reminder the sell was cohort-wide.

The day's biggest gainers ran on their own news. D. P. Abhushan rose 7.36% after June-quarter revenue jumped 58% and profit 77%, VA Tech Wabag added 6.32% on a large Bangalore water-treatment order, Tips Music gained 6.21% before a Wednesday board meeting on a possible buyback, and V-Mart Retail rose 5.21% on a 23% revenue update.
