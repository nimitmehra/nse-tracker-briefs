Markets gave back yesterday's IT rally in a single session.

Tata Consultancy Services fell about 8 percent. Tech Mahindra fell 6. HCL Tech fell 5.

The Nifty IT index closed down 5.6 percent — almost the entire two-day gain, gone.

There was no new bad news. The fall was the unwind of the previous day's narrow trade.

When a one-day rally is led by a single sector and one overnight catalyst, the position is fragile. Profit-takers wait. US bond yields backed up overnight. The Iran negotiation went quiet. Three small reasons added up to a clean exit.

The headline Nifty only dropped a third of a percent, because money rotated underneath. But IT-cohort holders had a hard day.
