On a quiet day, the small and mid-caps had room to run. A few names worth knowing.

PhysicsWallah rose 16 percent, the day's biggest move. The education company is dropping its plan to fund student loans itself and will partner with regulated lenders instead. The stock had fallen all week on worry about the in-house plan, and Thursday erased the whole loss in one session.

Anant Raj rose 8 percent, extending a run on a ₹25,000 crore deal to build data centres in Haryana.

Zen Technologies rose 7 percent on fresh defence orders.

Two of the three are riding the same theme: capital is hunting for companies with concrete, government-backed building plans.
