2026-07-10 | NSE Daily Brief: An earnings-led rally, carried by home money not foreign

## Description

Indian shares rose for a second straight session on Friday, the Nifty up just over one percent to 24,207, as the June-quarter earnings season opened well. A strong TCS read-through lit up IT, and PSU-bank beats led by Indian Bank opened the bank season on a confident note. Property was the best sector and the fear gauge fell to a one-year low. But the day's real tell was in the flows: domestic institutions carried the market up while foreign investors were net sellers, a correction to the week's "foreign money returning" story. Forward, two clocks: the below-normal monsoon and the 24-July US tariff cliff.
