## What Happened

### Garware Hi-Tech loses its chief financial officer

**Garware Hi-Tech Films, a portfolio holding, lost its chief financial officer Abhishek Agarwal on Friday evening.** The resignation landed at 17:06 IST — nine days after a record fourth-quarter print. Garware (GRWRHITECH) is the Aurangabad-based specialty-films maker. No successor was named in the Reg 30 — the Indian counterpart to a material-event filing. Authorised-person duties went to a whole-time director and the company secretary in the interim. The verbatim reason was the lawyered version familiar from a 2019 mid-cap unwind. The letter cited *"to pursue career opportunities outside the Company"* with explicit denial of *"any other material reason."* Three mitigators argue against the worst reading. The joint statutory auditors signed an unmodified opinion on May 6. The prior 180 days carry no audit-dialogue arc. And FY26 was the company's best year on record. A refreshed deep-research read is queued at priority nine.

**Three policy levers are now engaged at once, and the rupee still broke ₹96.** Indian Oil, Bharat Petroleum and Hindustan Petroleum raised retail petrol and diesel by ₹3 a litre on Friday — the first such move in more than four years.
