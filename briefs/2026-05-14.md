## What Happened

### Gold pays for the rupee, in ninety-six hours

**India more than doubled gold and silver import duties on Wednesday.** The Central Board of Indirect Taxes and Customs notified a fifteen-percent levy — ten percent basic customs plus five percent Agriculture Infrastructure Cess — up from six percent. Domestic gold futures spiked 7.2 percent intraday; silver 6.2 percent. The jewellery cohort took the visible hit, Kalyan Jewellers (KALYANKJIL) and the cluster around it extending a four-session bleed. ### Jio Financial converts the Allianz handshake into a corporate entity

**Jio Allianz General Insurance Limited was incorporated on Monday — the operationalisation of the April 22 partnership between Jio Financial Services (JIOFIN) and Allianz Europe.** Both partners hold fifty percent.
